# Miracle Center - Youth Mentorship Program

## Definition
Target: Youth and Young Adults of Miracle Center
Duration: 8weeks
Dates: April 19 - June 14 (flexible)
Age: 13 to 25
Theme: Identity
Bible verse: 1 Peter 2:9

## Structure:
- A mentee be assigned to a mentor (1:1)
- Study a bible character for 8weeks
- Memorize verses on Identity
-  Bible study/sharing
- Pray together 
- Ask feedback on Sunday message
- Meet for at least 1hour a week
- Mentees will be paired with a accountability squad (a group of 4)
- Develop relationship with God 
- Accountability partners meet once a week
- All mentors will meet at beginning and middle
- A 8 week study guide will be shared as a template
- Youth will plan the end of the celebration: after church June 14
